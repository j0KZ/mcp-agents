Design a database schema for an e-commerce platform

Requirements:
- Users can create accounts with email and password
- Users have profiles with name, address, and phone
- Products belong to categories
- Products have name, description, price, and stock quantity
- Customers can place orders containing multiple products
- Orders have status (pending, paid, shipped, delivered)
- Track order history and shipping addresses
- Support product reviews and ratings
- Maintain inventory levels
